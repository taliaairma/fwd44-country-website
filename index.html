Welcome to China

China is huge — we mean really huge. It's the fourth largest country on Earth, with deserts, rainforests, mountains, and megacities all sharing one time zone (yes, really).

This quick guide takes you to three corners of Chinese culture: jaw-dropping attractions (one for every season), mouth-awakening flavors (spicy, salty, sweet, sour), and wildlife you can't find anywhere else (hello, pandas).
