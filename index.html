CHINA

A journey through the Middle Kingdom

Welcome to China

China is land of dramatic contrasts — where ancient traditions meet futuristic cities, and where each season paints the landscape in a completely new palette. From the blossoms of spring in Jiuzhaigou to the ice sculptures of winter in Harbin, every season offers a unique reason to visit.

Use this guide to explore China's most breathtaking attractions, its diverse culinary traditions, and the unique wildlife that calls this land home.
